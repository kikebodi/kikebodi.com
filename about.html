About Me

I’m Kike Bodí, an AI Engineer passionate about building intelligent, high-performance, and privacy-conscious systems that merge human collaboration with advanced automation.

My journey began in Android engineering, where I designed robust mobile architectures with a focus on efficiency, scalability, and user experience. That same engineering discipline now fuels my work in AI Agent Development, LLM Engineering, and Transformer-based Systems — connecting reasoning, context, and execution into cohesive, reliable workflows.

I specialize in scalable AI automation, LLM fine-tuning, Agent orchestration, and custom retrieval pipelines for structured knowledge access. My stack includes Python, LangChain, Hugging Face, OpenAI API, and MCP-based infrastructure for model-centric backends. I focus on improving LLM reliability through structured reasoning, memory optimization, and context-window management in production environments.

My philosophy draws from sports and performance science — discipline, feedback loops, and data-driven optimization. I treat engineering like training: measure, iterate, recover, improve. The same principles that drive physical performance also guide AI system optimization.

I believe in human-centered automation — agents that protect focus, respect privacy, and amplify human capability. Whether it’s shipping production code, fine-tuning a model, or optimizing a reasoning pipeline, my goal is to deliver practical, measurable impact.
