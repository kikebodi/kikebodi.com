About Me

This is what I do.

“Most people overestimate what they can do in one year and underestimate what they can do in ten.”

-Bill Gates

10 years ago, I was an unskilled worker without studies. Now I have a Bachelor degree in Computer Science, founded two Startups and worked in high tech profiles.

I’m Kike Bodi, a software engineer and IT entrepreneur with more than 8 years in the sector.

I love to build new products and enjoy viewing the people using them. The most satisfying conversation I had was when a user of my Aluzz app (a Smartphone app to open doors) told me: “This is the future”. In short, this is what technology should do. To make processes more efficient and life easier.

I'm obsessed about sports, nutrition and health, creativity can’t flow in a stopped body.

I’m fascinated by the Startup world. It’s a very high demanding environment where you have to use not only the knowledge you have about technology but also in life.
